1st Perry Family Reunion

From the U.P. to St. Louis – four generations together.

In summer 2026, the children, grandchildren, great-grandchildren, and great-great-grandchildren of William Henry Perry Sr. and Irene "Irja" Marie (Hakala) Perry will gather in the St. Louis / O’Fallon, Missouri area to reconnect, remember, and make new memories.

Dates are still being finalized (likely Father’s Day weekend or July 4th weekend), but we’re beginning to plan now and would love to know if you’re hoping to attend.

Reunion Snapshot

  • Who: Descendants of William & Irene, plus spouses and partners
  • Where: St. Louis / O’Fallon, Missouri
  • When: Summer 2026 (exact weekend TBD)
  • Focus: Reconnecting, storytelling, and honoring loved ones
