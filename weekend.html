Reunion Weekend – Summer 2026

The final schedule will be shared as we get closer to the date, but here’s the general shape of the weekend we’re planning around the St. Louis / O’Fallon area.

Friday · Welcome Evening

  • Casual meet-and-greet
  • Snacks and visiting
  • Name tags & “Who’s who?” introductions
  • Old photos and stories from the U.P.

Saturday · Main Reunion Day

  • Group lunch or BBQ
  • Family photos by branch and generation
  • Kids’ activities and outdoor time
  • Story circle: “Growing Up Perry”

Sunday · Farewell & Free Time

  • Optional brunch
  • Time for St. Louis sightseeing
  • Relaxed visiting and goodbyes

Celebration of Life for John Alan Perry

During the reunion, we will set aside time for a Celebration of Life in honor of John Alan Perry (1942–2025), one of William and Irene’s sons and a deeply loved dad, grandpa, brother, and uncle.

This time will also be a space to remember other family members we’ve lost over the years, to share stories and photos, and to support one another as a family as we celebrate the lives that shaped us.

From Cornwall & Finland to the Upper Peninsula, to St. Louis and beyond.
