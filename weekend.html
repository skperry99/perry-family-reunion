Reunion Weekend – Summer 2026

The final schedule will be shared closer to the date, but here’s the general shape of the weekend we’re planning around the St. Louis / O’Fallon area.

Friday · Welcome Evening

  • Casual meet-and-greet
  • Snacks and visiting
  • Name tags & “Who’s who?” introductions
  • Old photos and stories from the U.P.

Saturday · Main Reunion Day

  • Group lunch or BBQ
  • Family photos by branch and generation
  • Kids’ activities and outdoor time
  • Story circle: “Growing Up Perry”

Sunday · Farewell & Free Time

  • Optional brunch
  • Time for St. Louis sightseeing
  • Relaxed visiting and goodbyes

Celebration of Life

During the reunion, we will set aside time for a Celebration of Life to honor and remember a beloved son, brother, and uncle who passed away in 2025, as well as other family members we’ve lost over the years.

This will be a space to share stories, photos, and memories, and to support one another as a family.

From Cornwall & Finland to the Upper Peninsula, to St. Louis and beyond.
