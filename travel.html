Travel & Lodging

Our home base for the reunion will be the St. Louis / O’Fallon, Missouri area. There are several hotels, restaurants, and family-friendly activities within a short drive of the main reunion location.

Getting Here

  • By Air: St. Louis Lambert International Airport (STL).
  • By Car: O’Fallon, MO is west of downtown St. Louis via major interstates (I-64/US-40 and I-70).
  • Exact addresses and directions for gathering spots will be shared directly with those who RSVP.

Where to Stay

There are several hotels within a short drive of the main reunion area. These options are all just off I-64/Highway 40 and close to restaurants, shopping, and family-friendly activities.

Nearby hotel options

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites St. Louis West–O’Fallon
    1175 Technology Dr, O’Fallon, MO 63368
    Standard rooms and suites with complimentary hot breakfast and Wi-Fi, located just off I-64/40 near local businesses and attractions.
  • Staybridge Suites O’Fallon Chesterfield (IHG)
    1155 Technology Dr, O’Fallon, MO 63368
    Extended-stay style suites with kitchenettes, free Wi-Fi, fitness center, and an outdoor pool. A good option for families who want more space or a longer stay.
  • Residence Inn by Marriott St. Louis O’Fallon
    101 Progress Point Ct, O’Fallon, MO 63368
    All-suite hotel with full kitchens, complimentary hot breakfast, indoor pool, and on-site laundry — nice for families or anyone staying several nights.
  • Hilton Garden Inn St. Louis / O’Fallon
    2310 Technology Dr, O’Fallon, MO 63368
    Hotel with restaurant, bar, indoor pool, and a local area shuttle, located near golf and upscale shopping.
  • Sleep Inn & Suites O’Fallon – Technology Drive
    1147 Technology Dr, O’Fallon, MO 63368
    Another nearby option on Technology Drive, with easy access to I-64/40 and the reunion area.

You’re welcome to stay anywhere that works best for you — these are simply nearby choices that should make it easy to get to and from the reunion events without a long drive.
